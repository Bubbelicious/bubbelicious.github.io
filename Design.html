NoWa - No Waste

An app born from a school project. We were a group of five which decided to concentrate on the theme of Sustainable Food Consumption. We went through 4 deliveries in this project: User research, Concept, Prototype and Iterations, all of which received the highest grade. I was nominated and named the design lead during the concept-delivery and held it through the rest of the project, and made most of the designs that we decided to use during the iterations. EU-Bike

A school project in which we were tasked with researching and testing the app to find out why it was so badly reviewed. We worked alone, but had sessions of brainstorming in groups occasionally. After analysing the data, we were to give our own suggestions of changes in the app that we thought would improve it, as well as presenting the ideas to our teachers while explaining our reasoning, like you would to a client. NärCon recrutement website

As a volunteer at NärCon’s events, I am very familiar with their recrutement website, as well as it’s flaws. It is an old site at this point, and while it works it is far from an effective or interactive design. I have taken upon myself to redesign the site so that it is easier to navigate and understand, as well as adapting it into a mobile friendly version. Something that the current website doesn’t have. I work on it during my free time, and have yet to present the idea to the people in charge, but I will update as it progresses. (More information in progress)

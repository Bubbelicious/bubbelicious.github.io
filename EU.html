EU- Bike

This school assignment consisted of doing user research, idea workshop, redesigning the app,
and then presenting the changes to "the client" (which in this case was played by our teacher).
Research and workshop was done in groups, while the redesigning phase was done individually.
Research Team: Maya Otterstål (me), Jessica Banefelt, Måns Järneström, Joel Öhrman

User research

The first thing we did once we got this assignment was to read the reviews from the app store, on how users thought about
using the app, and it was clear that the users were not happy with it. After testing the app ourself, we quickly
found several pain points in the user flow that should be retified. We tested the same user
flows on other people in our social surrounding and found that they had almost exactly the same problem
that we had with the app.
Using a customer journey map, we mapped the general flow and used the data we had recieved from our user testings to fill it out,
with very clear results.

Blaston

Blaston is a competetive, PVP dueling, VR game from Resolution Games.
You need to duck and avoid the slow bullets that your opponent are firing, at the same time as you fire back with as complex combinations you can possibly make. A fun work-out that is garanteed to get your heart racing.

Outside of the arena, there is an entire world that is reached though a door. Here you can find the Ozo lounge and socialize with the Blaston community, among other cool things.

For this game I worked on the UX design for the new campaign hand menu screen, that can be found on the arm of the player's avatar.

Requirements

To the left you can see some of Conor Souness' work on the UX design for the campaign in the main menu. My task for this project was to design the hand menu that would show the mission information of the campaign, located on the arm of the player's avatar.
The requirements for this task could be summarized like here:

  • Hand menu must contain all relevant mission information pertaining to the campaign.
  • When activated, the hand menu must not disturb the player's primary field of vision.
  • Hand menu must be easy to activate, read, and deactivate.
  • There must be a way for the player to know if there are updates in the missions.

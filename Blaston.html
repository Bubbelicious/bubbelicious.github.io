Blaston

Blaston is a competetive, PVP dueling, VR game from Resolution Games.
You need to duck and avoid the slow bullets that your opponent are firing, at the same time as you fire back with as complex combinations you can possibly make. A fun work-out that is garanteed to make your heart racing.

Outside of the arena, there is an entire world that is reached though a door. Here you can find the Ozo lounge and socialize with the Blaston community, among other cool things.

For this game I worked on the UX design for the new mission information screen, that can be found on the arm of the player's avatar.

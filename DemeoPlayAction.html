Play Actions

One of the functions that I worked on were making wireframes for the screens that are used when the player choses a Play action. The actions in question are: Join Game or Create Game, and the designs had to include a screen for joining a specific room, loading screens, and failed screens.
We also made sure to calculate the character limits for every screen to make it easier copywriting later.
